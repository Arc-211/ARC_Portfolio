Spoonful_Stories_Webd

Spoonful Stories is a React-based food blog platform designed for culinary enthusiasts who love exploring new recipes, engaging with a food-loving community, and enhancing their cooking skills. It offers a seamless, interactive experience, allowing users to browse recipes, register for cooking courses, submit reviews, and receive personalized culinary assistance via an AI-powered chatbox.This platform is built with a modern technology stack, featuring React for the frontend, Node.js and Express.js for the backend, and MongoDB for data storage. It integrates responsive layouts, secure user authentication, payment processing (Stripe API), and AI-driven assistance to provide a smooth and engaging user experience.
